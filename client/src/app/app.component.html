Privacy Policy

GENERAL

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy is intended to give you confidence in the privacy and security of the personal information we obtain when you use chordjamz.com.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

When you use our website information collected may include, username, chord documents and chord document metadata. By giving us this information, you will need to consent to our using it in the manner described in this policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by emailing us at admin@chordjamz.com. We will return or destroy your account and all associated data within five days of receipt of your withdrawal of consent.

USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We use personal information to allow us to customize your user experience and generating content for use by you and other users. By marking a chord sheet public you are giving consent for the associated data to be released publicly, including your username, time of file modification, version number, and chord sheet content.

DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We will not disclose your personal information to any third party under any circumstance unless you have consented to such disclosure or where we are required to do so by law. If we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, we may disclose your information to a relevant authority.

POLICY CHANGES

Changes in this policy will be posted on our website.You are advised to check our website regularly to view our most recent privacy policy.

Takeover and Dissolution

In the event of a takeover all data will be transferred to the new ownership. In the event of a dissolution all data will be destroyed.

If you are a copyright owner or an agent thereof and you believe that any content on chordjamz.com infringes upon your copyrights, you may submit a notice pursuant to the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (“DMCA”) by providing our Copyright Agent the following information in writing:
